I was born on the 27th of June, 2002 in Tuzla. I was a logic-driven but creative child, shy and introverted. While inherently introverted, I continued pursuing sports and creative arts, such as dancing and painting. I won multiple awards in Latic and Standard dances, literature contests, art contests and theater plays. Playing instruments was one of my main hobbies, as well as language learning. I graduated both middle school and high school as an A student.

During my high school years, I developed an interest in psychology, sociology and Latin studies. One of my greatest accomplishments is a gold ECCL Euroclassica Vestibulum certificate in Latin. With the help of my psychology and sociology professors, I wrote several psychology and sociology research papers, one of which I included in the "Works" section of this portfolio.
